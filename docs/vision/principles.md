# Principles

- **Autonomy with auditability**: Full freedom to execute, with an unbreakable record of every action.
- **Flexibility with guardrails**: Adaptable to any task while respecting ethical and operational boundaries.
- **Progress through decomposition**: Complex missions are broken down into manageable, verifiable steps.
- **Reliability through verification**: Every step is checked for quality before moving forward.
- **Continuous Evolution**: The agent learns from every mission, improving its tools and tactics autonomously.
