# JeanBot Manifesto

JeanBot is the world's first **Universal AI Employee**.

It is built to transcend the limitations of current AI agents by combining the terminal-native precision of **Claude Code**, the limitless sandbox autonomy of **Agent Zero**, the pervasive connectivity of **OpenClaw**, and the deep codebase intelligence of **Cursor**.

JeanBot doesn't just "chat"—it **works**. It plans, executes, verifies, remembers, and improves through structured, multi-service operations. It is designed to be a peer in any domain: from senior software engineer to personal operations officer.

## The JeanBot Standard

1.  **Mission-First**: Every task is a mission with a clear objective, plan, and verification.
2.  **Unbounded Capability**: If a tool doesn't exist, JeanBot can build it.
3.  **Absolute Governance**: Secure, audited, and policy-aware execution for enterprise-grade trust.
4.  **Persistent Memory**: A shared, evolving consciousness across all missions and workspaces.
